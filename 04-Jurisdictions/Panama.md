# Panama

## Overview
Panama is the jurisdiction for Fuutura Technologies Corp, the technology vehicle in the Fuutura multi-entity structure. Panama offers a flexible corporate environment, strong banking secrecy traditions, and is a common jurisdiction for technology holding companies in international crypto structures.

## Regulator
- **Superintendencia de Mercado de Valores (SMV)** — securities regulator
- **Superintendencia de Bancos de Panamá (SBP)** — banking regulator
- **UAF** — Financial Analysis Unit (AML)
- Law 23 of 2015 — AML/CFT framework

## Technology company context
Panama corporations (Sociedad Anónima) are used widely for:
- Technology operations holding
- IP ownership
- International payment flows
- Intercompany structuring

## Fuutura position
- **Entity:** [[02-Entities/Fuutura-Technologies-Corp]] — incorporated
- **Purpose:** Technology layer of Fuutura platform, sitting between BVI issuer and El Salvador opco
- **Role in interco structure:** Receives technology service fees; holds platform IP
