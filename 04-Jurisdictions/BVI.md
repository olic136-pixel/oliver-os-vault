# BVI

## Overview
The British Virgin Islands is the jurisdiction for Fuutura Treasury Inc, the $FTRA token issuer. The BVI is a leading offshore jurisdiction for token issuance structures due to its flexible company law, established trust law framework, and absence of corporate income tax.

## Regulator
- **BVI Financial Services Commission (FSC)** — financial services regulation
- **BVI International Tax Authority** — tax matters
- BVI Business Companies Act 2004 — company law framework

## Token issuance in BVI
The BVI is commonly used for token issuance vehicles:
- BVI Business Company (BC) — most common structure
- No specific crypto-asset licensing regime for pure utility token issuers
- Utility token opinion required to confirm non-securities status
- AML obligations under the Anti-Money Laundering and Terrorist Financing Code
