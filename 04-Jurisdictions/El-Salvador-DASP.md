# El Salvador — DASP

## Overview
El Salvador is the jurisdiction for Fuutura's operating entity and exchange licence. El Salvador enacted the Bitcoin Law in 2021 making it legal tender, and subsequently established the Digital Asset Service Provider (DASP) licensing framework administered by the Banco Central de Reserva (BCR) and Comisión Nacional de Activos Digitales (CNAD).

## Regulator
- **CNAD** — Comisión Nacional de Activos Digitales (National Commission for Digital Assets)
- **BCR** — Banco Central de Reserva (Central Reserve Bank)
- **BSP** — Banco de El Salvador / Banking regulator context

## DASP licence framework
The DASP licence permits operation as a digital asset service provider in El Salvador:
- Exchange services
- Custody
- Transfer and settlement
- Bitcoin Lightning Network operations
- AML/CFT obligations under El Salvador law
