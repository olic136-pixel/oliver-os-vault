# UK — FCA and CQC

## Overview
The United Kingdom regulatory environment is relevant to Oliver's work across two distinct regulatory bodies: the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) for financial services, and the Care Quality Commission (CQC) for health and social care. These are separate regulators with separate frameworks — combined here as a single UK regulatory profile note.

---

## Section A — FCA (Financial Conduct Authority)

### Overview
The FCA regulates financial services firms and markets in the UK. Relevant to Libertas Chambers (advising on financial crime matters) and potentially to Qlarif/Human8 if any financial services components emerge.

### Key framework
- **Financial Services and Markets Act 2000 (FSMA)**
- **FCA Handbook** — COBS, PRIN, SUP, MAR, COND
- **Money Laundering Regulations 2017** — AML/CFT
- **Proceeds of Crime Act 2002** — confiscation proceedings (Oliver as barrister)

### Relevance to Oliver's work
- Criminal defence in financial crime matters (market abuse, fraud, money laundering)
- POCA confiscation proceedings
- Regulatory advice context (Qanun product — FCA framework included in coverage)

## Section B — CQC (Care Quality Commission)

### Overview
The CQC regulates health and social care in England. **Qlarifai Care's customers** are CQC-regulated care home operators. Qlarifai Care is not itself regulated by CQC — it builds AI compliance tools for CQC-regulated entities.

### Key framework
- **Health and Social Care Act 2008** — CQC's primary enabling legislation
- **Care Quality Commission (Registration) Regulations 2009**
- **Fundamental Standards** (Regulations 9–20A) — the standards CQC inspects against
- **Key Lines of Enquiry (KLOEs)** — inspection framework (Safe, Effective, Caring, Responsive, Well-led)
- **KLOE successor framework** — updated inspection methodology

### Relevance to Qlarifai
Qlarifai Care's AI assistant helps care home operators:
- Assess SOPs against CQC Fundamental Standards
- Identify gaps and improvement areas
- Generate improved SOP drafts
- Prepare for CQC inspections
