# DIFC / DFSA

## Overview
Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC) is Dubai's international financial centre, regulated by the Dubai Financial Services Authority (DFSA). Key jurisdiction for the Dubai Law Firm and for Qanun's product coverage.

## Regulator
- **DFSA** — Dubai Financial Services Authority
- English common law framework (independent of UAE civil law)
- Issues licences across financial services, legal, and professional services

## Key regulatory framework
- **DFSA Rulebook** — comprehensive regulatory framework
- **Law on the Application of Civil and Commercial Laws in the DIFC**
- **DIFC Employment Law**
- **DFSA Conduct of Business Module (COB)**
- **Prudential — Investment, Insurance Intermediation and Banking (PIB)**

## Law firm licensing in DIFC
The DIFC permits law firms to operate within the centre under DIFC law. Relevant for the Dubai Law Firm's DIFC presence:
- DIFC registered law firm
- Foreign law firm recognition
- Obligations under DIFC legal professional rules

## Virtual assets in DIFC
DFSA has developed a virtual asset regulatory framework. Relevant to Qanun's product positioning:
- Digital asset licences under DFSA framework
- Intersection with VARA (Dubai mainland) for cross-jurisdiction analysis
