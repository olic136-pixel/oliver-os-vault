# ADGM / FSRA

## Overview
Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM) is Abu Dhabi's international financial centre, regulated by the Financial Services Regulatory Authority (FSRA). It is a critical jurisdiction across multiple Oliver projects — Fuutura's exchange licensing target, Qanun's primary product market, and the destination for Oliver's September 2026 relocation.

## Regulator
- **FSRA** — Financial Services Regulatory Authority
- Operates under ADGM's English common law framework
- Issues Financial Services Permissions (FSPs) across five prudential categories

## Key regulatory framework
- **FSMR** — Financial Services and Markets Regulations 2015
- **PRU Rulebook** — Prudential categories 1–5
- **COBS** — Conduct of Business Rulebook
- **GEN** — General Module
- **AML Rulebook** — Anti-Money Laundering
- **Virtual Asset Framework (FSRA)** — crypto/VASP regulation

## Virtual asset regulation
ADGM/FSRA has an established virtual asset framework. Key for Fuutura Exchange licensing:
- VASP licence (Virtual Asset Service Provider)
- Accepted Virtual Assets list
- AML/CFT requirements for VASPs
- Capital requirements by category
