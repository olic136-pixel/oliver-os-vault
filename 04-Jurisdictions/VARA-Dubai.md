# VARA — Dubai

## Overview
The Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority (VARA) is Dubai's standalone virtual asset regulator, established under Law No. 4 of 2022. VARA regulates virtual asset activities across Dubai (excluding DIFC, which is regulated by DFSA). Relevant to Qanun's product coverage and potentially to the Dubai Law Firm's advisory practice.

## Regulator
- **VARA** — Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority
- Established: 2022
- Scope: Dubai emirate (mainland + some free zones, excluding DIFC)
- Headquarters: Dubai World Trade Centre Authority (DWTCA) free zone

## VARA licensing framework
VARA issues licences across a range of virtual asset activities:
- **VA Issuance Services** — token issuance
- **VA Broker-Dealer Services**
- **VA Exchange Services**
- **VA Lending and Borrowing Services**
- **VA Management and Investment Services**
- **VA Transfer and Settlement Services**
- **VA Custody Services**
