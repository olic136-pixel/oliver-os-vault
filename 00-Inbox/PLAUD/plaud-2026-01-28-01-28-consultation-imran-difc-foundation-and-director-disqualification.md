## Background
Imran aims to establish a family foundation in the Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC). The initial structure will consist of the foundation and one Prescribed Company (PC) intended to hold property. He has a historical issue: a **voluntary director’s disqualification in the UK from about 12 years ago**, stemming from a VAT-related matter with a company he was involved with. There were no criminal proceedings or findings of fact, and he is currently pursuing legal action to have the disqualification overturned. This disqualification appears on background checks like World-Check. He wants to understand if this will block him from acting as the founder of the new DIFC foundation.
## Pain Points
The primary concern is that a **past voluntary director’s disqualification from the UK, which occurred approximately 12 years ago, might prevent him from successfully setting up a DIFC foundation with himself as the founder.** This disqualification, although voluntary and related to a VAT issue with no financial loss to the exchequer, remains on his record and appears on **World-Check**.
* **Impact:** This could block the entire foundation setup process or force a change in the intended structure, such as appointing another family member as the founder, which is viable but less preferred.
* **Current Situation:** The issue is being raised proactively before starting the application. He is suing the lawyers who gave the original advice and is confident the disqualification will be overturned, but this process takes time and the disqualification is currently still active.
* **Context:** His business interests are not in the UK, so the disqualification does not impact current operations. The goal is to set up a family foundation, and the concern is about the application and approval process with the DIFC authorities.
* **Stakeholders:** Imran is the key stakeholder and wishes to be the founder. Family members are involved as potential alternative founders and beneficiaries.
## Expectations

* **Success Metrics:** Successful registration of the foundation and the PC, regardless of who is named the founder, and clarity on the process from the start to avoid issues during the application.
