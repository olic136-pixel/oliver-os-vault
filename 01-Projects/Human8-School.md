# Human8 — Blue Ocean Academy (School Project)

## Overview
Blue Ocean Academy is a Muslim school project in Liverpool — the primary education initiative under Humanate's scope. Ismail is the lead operational figure. Oliver is involved as legal/structuring adviser. Gabe is involved in system design (STL framework). The project is in fundraising and structure-building phase.

## Status
**Active — fundraising phase. Freehold acquisition is the immediate priority (deadline: July 2026 to avoid price increase).**

## Fundraising
- **Target:** £1.5m for freehold + ~£300k costs
- **Model:** Debt financing — investors hold a first charge against HoldCo asset (not equity)
- **Return:** ~10% per annum
- **Exit:** Year 5 — investors bought out, refinanced with cheaper institutional capital
- **Ticket size:** Minimum £100k — sophisticated/HNW investors only
- **Sharia-compliant options:** Murabaha, musharaka being explored

## STL framework
Gabe proposed the Amazon "Single-Threaded Lever" model for roles and accountability:
- Each person owns a thread (goal, beneficiary, definition of done, decision rights, metrics, cadence, exit criteria)
- Equal share structure agreed for all partners
- Shareholder agreement to be built around STL outputs
- Performance review every 6 months
