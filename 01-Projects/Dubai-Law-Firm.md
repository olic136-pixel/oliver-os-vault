# Dubai Law Firm

## Overview
A new UAE law firm to be launched by Oliver and IK, with a 6-month horizon from April 2026. Licensed across Dubai onshore and DIFC, with an expansion route to ADGM via an Emirati partner. The firm will leverage Oliver's EncroChat/encrypted communications expertise and multi-jurisdiction regulatory depth.
