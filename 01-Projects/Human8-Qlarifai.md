# Qlarifai — CQC Compliance AI Assistant

## Overview
Qlarifai is an AI assistant for CQC-regulated care home operators in England. It assesses and improves their Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) to ensure compliance with CQC Fundamental Standards. The initial vertical is care homes. A Dubai collaboration thread is nascent. Qlarifai is not itself CQC regulated — it is a compliance enablement tool for regulated entities.

## Product
**Qlarifai** — AI assistant for CQC-regulated care home operators:
- Uploads and assesses existing SOPs against CQC Fundamental Standards
- Identifies compliance gaps
- Generates improved SOP drafts
- Prepares care homes for CQC inspection
- Initial vertical: care homes → planned expansion to other CQC-regulated sectors

## Regulatory context (customers, not Qlarifai)
Qlarifai's customers operate under:
- CQC Fundamental Standards (Regulations 9–20A)
- Key Lines of Enquiry (KLOEs) — Safe, Effective, Caring, Responsive, Well-led
- Health and Social Care Act 2008
