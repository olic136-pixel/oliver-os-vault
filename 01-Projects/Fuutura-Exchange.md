# Fuutura Exchange

## Overview
Fuutura Exchange is a regulated cryptocurrency exchange being built across three jurisdictions — El Salvador (DASP operating licence), BVI (token issuer), and Panama (technology vehicle). The exchange is targeting a launch horizon of approximately 6 months from April 2026. The critical path item is securing an El Salvador operating partner.

## Current focus
**El Salvador operating partner search** — this is the single most important unresolved item for the Fuutura launch. Torres Legal is actively assisting. No El Salvador entity exists yet — it is in formation pending the operating partner.
