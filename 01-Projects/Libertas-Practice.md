# Libertas Practice

## Overview
Oliver's barrister practice at Libertas Chambers. Primary professional identity and income source. Specialisms: complex financial crime, encrypted communications (EncroChat — leading UK practitioner), digital evidence. All criminal matters are listed in the Libertas Diary and flow into the vault via Google Calendar MCP.

## Specialism
- **EncroChat and encrypted communications** — leading UK practitioner; strategic entry point for international work
- **Complex financial crime** — fraud, money laundering, POCA confiscation
- **Digital evidence** — admissibility, forensic analysis, expert evidence

## Diary integration
- **Libertas Diary** — lexhosted.com — Google Calendar integration active
- Each hearing generates a calendar event → vault picks up via GCal MCP
- PLAUD device captures hearing transcripts → syncs to matter sub-folder
- Pre-hearing briefs generated automatically 30 minutes before each listing

## Matters
All active matters are indexed at [[08-Matters/_index]]. Each matter has its own sub-folder:
- `_index.md` — matter overview, charges, representation
- `Hearings.md` — hearing log
- Linked PLAUD transcripts
- Evidence and instructions notes
