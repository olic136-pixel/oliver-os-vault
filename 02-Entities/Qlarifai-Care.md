## Purpose
Qlarifai Care is the UK operating entity for the Qlarifai product. It is **not itself CQC regulated** — it builds and sells an AI assistant to CQC-regulated care home operators. The product assesses and improves their SOPs to ensure CQC compliance. Initial vertical: care homes. Dubai collaboration thread under development.

## Product
**Qlarifai** — AI assistant for CQC-regulated care home operators:
- Assesses existing SOPs against CQC standards
- Identifies gaps and improvement areas
- Generates improved SOP drafts
- Care home vertical first; expansion planned
